About Gut Healing Lab

Gut Healing Lab was founded in 2026 with a single mission: to bridge the gap between cutting-edge microbiome research and everyday digestive health.

We analyze hundreds of peer-reviewed studies each month and distill them into clear, actionable protocols. Every article on this site is fact-checked against primary literature and written to empower you to make informed decisions about your gut health.

Our content covers SIBO, leaky gut, candida, histamine intolerance, low FODMAP diet, probiotics, and the gut-brain axis. We do not promote quick fixes — we promote protocols backed by reproducible science.
